Super Pizza by Super Plumbers!

Our History

After growing tired of plumbing, chasing giant turtles, princesses, and stomping (literally) on various forms of protected wildlife from mysteriously unsupported, yet floating bricks, brothers Mario and Luigi decided it was time to pursue their true passion: pizza! Now they offer you the greatest in pizza and pizza related products!

Setting up shop in beautiful Winter Park, FL, the brothers have honed their craft, using only the finest ingredients and making all of their menu items in house when possible. If you're in need of a power-up, you've come to the right place!
